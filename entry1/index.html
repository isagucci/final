Middle Childhood

6 - 9 Years

Gradual stabilization of growth rates, development of permanent teeth, and further refinement of motor skills.

Influence of gender expectations on clothing choices and behaviors, with girls often encouraged to wear dresses or feminine attire and exhibit behaviors deemed "ladylike" or nurturing.

Expansion of social networks, increasing independence from caregivers, and development of cognitive abilities such as reasoning and problem-solving.
