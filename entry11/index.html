Late Older Adulthood

80 - 89 Years

Greater frailty and vulnerability to illness, increased dependency on others for daily activities, sensory impairments such as hearing and vision loss.

Potential isolation or loneliness, reliance on support from caregivers or community services, limited mobility impacting social interactions.

Coping with loss of independence, adjustment to changes in health and mobility, potential sense of fulfillment from life experiences and relationships.
