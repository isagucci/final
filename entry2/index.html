Pre-Adolescence

10 - 12 Years

Onset of puberty with the development of secondary sexual characteristics, such as breast development and growth of pubic hair.

Peer pressure and media influence leading to the adoption of specific clothing styles and behaviors conforming to societal norms of femininity.

Heightened self-awareness, body image concerns, and emerging interest in romantic relationships and sexuality.
