Early Adulthood

23 - 29 Years

Peak physical health and fertility, stability in hormone levels, and optimal cognitive functioning.

Balancing professionalism in the workplace with personal style, pressure to maintain a youthful appearance while projecting maturity and competence.

Establishment of career, financial independence, consideration of long-term commitments such as marriage and parenthood.
