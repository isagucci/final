Late Adolescence

18 - 22 Years

Completion of physical growth, reproductive system fully matured, and establishment of adult bone density.

Navigating tension between societal expectations of modesty and self-expression, experimenting with personal style while conforming to cultural norms.

Transition to adulthood, pursuit of higher education or career goals, and establishment of intimate relationships.
