Early Old Adulthood

60 - 69 Years

Gradual decline in physical strength and stamina, increased susceptibility to chronic health conditions such as cardiovascular disease and osteoporosis.

Retirement planning, pursuit of leisure activities, potential adjustment to empty nest syndrome.

Coping with changes in physical health, pursuit of personal interests, and potential reflections on life achievements and regrets.
