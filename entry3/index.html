Adolescence

13 - 17 Years

Continued physical growth and maturation, menarche (onset of menstruation), and hormonal fluctuations.

Heightened pressure to conform to idealized standards of beauty and femininity, leading to increased emphasis on appearance and adherence to fashion trends for social acceptance.

Identity formation, peer influence, emotional volatility, and exploration of future goals and aspirations..
