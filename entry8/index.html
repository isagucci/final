Late Middle Adulthood

50 - 59 Years

Menopause typically occurs, marking the end of reproductive capacity.

Women may experience societal perceptions of invisibility as they age, leading to a sense of liberation from traditional beauty standards and a greater emphasis on comfort and practicality in clothing choices.

Adjustment to menopausal symptoms, concerns about aging and mortality, potential caregiving responsibilities for aging parents.
