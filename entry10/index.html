Middle Older Adulthood

70 - 79 Years

Further decline in physical health and mobility, increased prevalence of age-related conditions such as arthritis and cognitive decline.

Adjustment to retirement lifestyle, potential grief from loss of friends and family, reflection on life achievements and regrets.

Coping with changes in physical health and mobility, maintaining social connections, and finding purpose and meaning in life.
