Prime Adulthood

30 - 39 Years

Maintenance of physical health with slight declines in metabolic rate and muscle mass, increased risk of fertility decline.

Expectations to dress professionally while expressing personal style, balancing femininity with professionalism in fashion choices.

Balancing career and family responsibilities, reevaluation of life goals and priorities, potential mid-life crises.
